Frequently Asked Questions

How can I place an order?

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.

Can I cancel or modify my order after it's been placed?

You can cancel or modify your order within 24 hours of placing it. Please contact our customer service team as soon as possible with your order details.

What are your shipping options and costs?

We offer standard and expedited shipping options. Shipping costs vary based on the delivery method and destination. You can view the shipping charges at checkout.

How long will it take to receive my order?

Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days, while expedited shipping takes 2-3 business days. Delivery times may vary based on your location.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship internationally. Shipping times and costs for international orders will be calculated at checkout.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn and undamaged shoes in their original packaging. To initiate a return, please visit our Returns page and follow the instructions.

How do I exchange a product?

To exchange a product, please return the original item for a refund and place a new order for the desired item. Follow our return process for the initial item.

When will I receive my refund?

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect your returned item. The refund will be issued to the original payment method.

How do I know what size to order?

Please refer to our Size Guide, which provides detailed measurements and conversion charts to help you choose the correct size. If you're unsure, our customer service team can assist you.

What should I do if my shoes don't fit properly?

If your shoes don't fit, you can return them for a refund or exchange within 30 days. Ensure the shoes are unworn and in their original packaging when returning them.

Are your shoes true to size?

Our shoes generally run true to size, but fit may vary slightly by style and brand. We recommend checking our Size Guide for specific information.

What materials are your shoes made from?

Our shoes are made from high-quality materials such as leather, synthetic fabrics, and textiles. Each product description provides detailed information about the materials used.

How do I care for my shoes?

Shoe care instructions vary by material. For leather shoes, use a quality leather cleaner and conditioner. For fabric shoes, spot clean with a mild detergent. Always refer to the care instructions provided with your shoes.

Do you offer any discounts or promotions?

Yes, we offer seasonal sales and special promotions. Sign up for our newsletter to stay updated on the latest deals.

How can I contact customer service?

You can contact our customer service team via email at support@shoeshop.com or by phone at 1-800-123-4567. Our team is available Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM (EST).

Do you have a physical store location?

Yes, we have several physical store locations. Please visit our Store Locator page to find the nearest store to you.
